2015 Ford Mustang GT SEMA display car for Baer Brakes Featuring parts from Vortec Supercharger, Ridetech, adjustable suspension and more.

Before I go into what makes this car special I want to make sure you know that is is a one owner Arizona car. It is free of rust. It has had no paint work. The Carfax is clean. This is a beautiful well maintained Mustang GT worthy of a look even with out all of the upgrades.

As with many over the top builds done by manufactures for SEMA this Mustang although dressed in a subtle white is a monster on the road or the track. Power tuned conservatively for dependability by Xtreme Motorsports of Tempe Arizona produced 508.01 horsepower at the rear wheels and 389.11 foot lbs of torque (chart is available). These figures were solid enough to propel the car to 153 miles per hour in the Gila Bend AZ No Fly Zone flying mile event.

This Mustang has been a feature car in several magazines, promotional videos, and promotional print advertisements. Baer, Vortec, and Ridetech This video shows the car in its glory.

The total cost of the build in parts alone is in excess of $21,000. This would be plus the labor.

I have driven the car extensively. It performs fantastically. It has a great exhaust note that you would expect from a 5.0. The Vortec wine is so intoxicating as you get into the throttle. The day I took these pictures it was over 108 degrees. The motor ran cool and the A/C blew cold. The adjustable suspension was on its lowest setting which can make some noise when pushed hard, which I did. Please take a look at the below items that have been installed on this car.
