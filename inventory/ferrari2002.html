2002 Ferrari 360 Spider F1 Freshly Serviced

Description:

2002 Ferrari 360 Spider : Unusual as this might be this Ferrari has actually been driven. With that said it has been serviced well. It has just completed the recommended and then some Ferrari service. This is one of the more desirable “later” cars produced after 2002. The soft points being the clutch which is operating perfectly, and service upkeep which this one has just received. Also important is that this Ferrari is stock with the exception of the exhaust which is aftermarket. I do not see a brand designation on the exhaust. I do not believe it is a Tubi brand. I will detail the condition below:

Exterior: Original paint with no signs of paint work. Minor rub marks where the Top rubs. The top is in good condition. Operates perfectly. there is a video in the google album of it raising and lowering. The front windshield trim is rubbed through the black anodizing. Minor scratches and chips are present and commiserate of a car this age.

Interior: No strange odors. I did drive it with the top down, but I did not detect any. The switch gear is in good shape. No stickiness. The paddle shifters have clearly been used. The anodizing is rubbed off. The drivers side seat bolster shows sign of wear. No holes. The audio system has been updated to a Pioneer head unit.

Engine: Clean and and well serviced. Runs and drives great.

Chassis: No rust, New Michelin tires on factory alloy wheels.

Purchased new June 20th 2002 in Arizona this Ferrari split its time between Newport Beach and Phoenix. The Carfax tracks each service in the following order with the exemption of the service just completed at FMS Ferrari in Fountain Hills Arizona. The receipt from FMS is in the Gallery. the owners name and dollar amount are blanked out,but you can see how comprehensive the service was. The total cost with tires exceeded 9500.
