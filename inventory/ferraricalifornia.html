Ferrari California 2010

Description:


2010 Ferrari California Originally purchased in Scottsdale Arizona where it has been since it was new. The condition of the car is excellent. I took the pictures with out being detailed to show its true condition. I will document the flaws I found during my inspection below. A service was completed in 2017 at 6886 miles. We are happy to bring it into Ferrari to have it inspected for a buyer. Buyer will need to pay for the inspection.

Body: There are very small road imperfections on the nose as well as a few small chips in the paint. The windshield has a small nick that I have documented in the flicker album. The seal area where the top meets the body has scuffing also shown on the gallery.

Interior: Very good shape. No stickiness of the switches, there are minor rubs on the steering column by the key,. The back panels have what appears to be small splotching of color. They look like they can be wiped off and maybe they can with the correct solvent. Very small scuffs on the drivers side seat bolster.

Chassis: the bottom of the car is clean as a 9500 mile Arizona car is expected to be. There is considerable rubbing of the plastic under fairing on the front apron. it is not cracked. The rest of the bottom is a wash away from new condition.

Engine compartment: Excellent original condition. No slimy dressing from a detail shop.

Miscellaneous : We only have 1 key. All books and manuals as well as the tool kit are included. We have the copies of the previous service records also.

Operation and Drivability: The car drives perfectly. The large wheels that are on the car have new tires. The original wheels have the original tires still mounted and come with the car.
