One owner purchased new in Arizona, where it remains today. Very low 15,695 original Miles. I will document the condition of this 911 in detail below, but understand this is a very low mileage un-touched undetailed car.

Mechanically: This 911 drives like it is new. The clutch is perfect, and the suspension is free of noises.
